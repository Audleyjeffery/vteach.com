At vteach, we contribute to the infrastructure of the society through our platform where we pay both employed or unemployed, teachers of different skills or teachers of different subjects, certificated or uncertificated teachers for every service rendered. We also furthermore contribute by paying students for each service they purchase thus creating a balance and reducing the rate for unemployment in our society.

About us

Vteach is an online learning platform based in Lagos, Nigeria, founded in the year 2022. Vteach is involved in the connecting of teachers and learners via video chat in order to ease interactions and understanding. Vteach also help the teachers and students earn or create a source of income through teaching and learning at their own comfort, anyday, anytime.

Our plans

Here at Vteach, we have different plans that suits your budget capabilities, These plans are for the learners account. The plan varies per the length of the class.

Plan A
30mins

This plan is the only free plan in the plans library, it gives access to a free 30 minutes class without you paying a dime. It has no limited features. At the end of the 30mins, the video call would be terminated.

Plan B
1hour

This plan is not a free plan. It is the cheapest plan in the library. It gives access to an hour class with the payment range of N2000 - N4000. It also have no limited feature. At the end of the 1hour, the video call would be terminated.

Plan C
1hr30mins

This plan is not a free plan. It gives access to an hour and 30 minutes class with the payment range of N4000 - N6000. It also have no limited feature. At the end of the 1hour 30 minutes, the video call would be terminated.

Plan D
2hours

This plan is not a free plan. It gives access to a 2hours class with the payment range of N6000 - N8000. It also have no limited feature. At the exhaustion of the 2hours, the video call would be terminated.

Our services

At vteach, we pay students 20% of the amount used to purchase a class with more than 30 minutes duration with a teacher.We also pay the students with referral bonuses and we also pay them another bonus price after they have bought up to a certain number of classes with more than 30 minutes duration. While we pay the teachers 50% of the money a student purchased his/her class with. the company then takes the remaining 30% for its continous functionality.
